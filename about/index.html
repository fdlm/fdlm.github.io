About Me

I am a PhD researcher at the Department of Computational Perception of the Johannes Kepler University in Linz, where I work on probabilistic models and deep neural networks for audio/music processing. Currently I am interested in extracting musical artifacts such as (down-)beats and chords/harmonies from audio recordings to faciliate a richer computational understanding of music.
